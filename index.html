About

The logic behind the person

I grew up in Hong Kong and came to the UK to study pharmacy. Across every domain I've worked in, the same logic applies: understand the system, find the edge, act with precision.

The NHS sharpened that instinct. When the margin for error is zero, you learn to think clearly under pressure — to cut through noise, size up risk quickly, and commit to a decision. I carried that discipline into markets. My trading framework is systematic and risk-first — I think in probabilities, not predictions. Every trade starts with the downside.

Building — born from curiosity, forged by tenacity — a drive to explore new domains and create things that actually add value. No computer science background, no formal training. Just a problem worth solving, AI as the force multiplier, and the satisfaction of shipping something that works.
